Our Ministries

Youth Ministry

Our Youth Ministry is dedicated to nurturing and empowering the younger generation with the teachings of Christ. We organize regular Bible study sessions, youth camps, and community service activities.

Women’s Fellowship

The Women’s Fellowship focuses on encouraging and supporting women in their spiritual journey. We meet regularly for prayer sessions, Bible studies, and workshops aimed at empowering women in all aspects of life.

Children’s Ministry

The Children’s Ministry offers a nurturing environment where young ones can learn about God’s love and grow in their faith. Our activities include Bible stories, interactive lessons, and fun events designed to engage children in their spiritual journey.

Men’s Ministry

The Men’s Ministry focuses on encouraging and supporting men in their spiritual journey. We meet regularly for prayer sessions, Bible studies, and workshops aimed at empowering men in all aspects of life.

About Us

Christ Living Faith Miracle Ministry is dedicated to spreading the love and teachings of Jesus Christ. We are committed to our local community and beyond.
