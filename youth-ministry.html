Youth Ministry

Welcome to the Youth Ministry of Christ Living Faith Miracle Ministry! We are a vibrant community of young individuals passionate about faith, service, and making a positive impact.

Our Mission

To empower young people to live a Christ-centered life, foster spiritual growth, and engage in meaningful service within the church and community.

Our Vision

To build a vibrant and supportive environment where youth can develop their faith, leadership skills, and purpose in life.
