Our Services

Discover our weekly services and join us in worship and community.

Monday Prayer Meetings

Join us every Monday at 6:00 PM for an uplifting time of prayer and intercession. Our prayer meetings are designed to help you connect with God and find spiritual strength.

Wednesday Bible Studies

Our Bible studies take place every Wednesday at 7:00 PM. Engage in in-depth study and discussion of Scripture to deepen your understanding and grow in faith.

Sunday Services

Experience our main worship service every Sunday at 10:00 AM. Join us for dynamic worship, inspiring sermons, and a vibrant community atmosphere.

Get Involved

We invite you to be part of our services and community. For more information or to get involved, please contact us.
